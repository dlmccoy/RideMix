Mission

RideMix aims to provide personalized recommendations for the group of people in your car! We gather information from all your different social networks including Facebook, Yelp, and Foursquare to provide the best suggestions for everyone

What We Do

RideMix is a web application that provides the user with real-time suggestions for things to do based on your current location. We generate different results for every group of people, providing a unique experience every time. In addition to places information, we also generate different conversation topics for people in the car to alleviate any awkward car rides and trips.

Who We Are

We are a group of Stanford students and RideMix is our product for our CS 210 class. We have been working on this product for the past two quarters with our corporate sponsor Volkswagen.
